Purgatorium - Hellbound

Abandon all hope those who enter here. Prepare to face all your nightmares using your only obstacle as the only weapon you have!

Prepare Your Soul. Prepare Your Brain. But Above All Prepare Yourself.

Purgatorium - Hellbound is an FPS Puzzle Solving Horror game where you have to use your puzzle solving skills to get through the several levels of the game while trying to figure out what happened to your house and wife. Who are you? Dave, the one person that is trapped in the middle of this messy situation, completely unaware of what has happened to anything at all.

Some Say It's For Science. Others Might Say We're Just Messing With Your Mind

While developing the game the team had their minds set on one specific goal, among the many we drew for this game. That goal was no more no less than to play with your mind, to explore its weaknesses, to explore your fears and trust in yourself and in the envirornment around you. Lets see how good of a puzzle solver you are when the only thing you can use to help you solve a puzzle, your mind, is under such a pressure from your fears.

Still Think You're Strong Enough To Face This?

This game was especailly designed in order to torture your senses in the best possible way. Think we're mean? Just remember we could have made this game in VR but we decided that not even as it stands there is anyone capable of easily beating it. Care to prove us wrong?
